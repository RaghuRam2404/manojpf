What This Work Taught Me

Skills that still show up in every product I design

Typography systems

Building brand type hierarchies from scratch taught me how to create purposeful, readable type in complex SaaS interfaces, where hierarchy carries meaning, not just style.

Colour & visual language

Building brand palettes gave me a sharp eye for colour contrast, accessibility, and visual consistency at scale — skills that directly inform how I approach design tokens today.

First impressions as a discipline

Brand design is about communicating a feeling instantly. That same discipline — "what does this say in the first 2 seconds?" — applies to every UI decision I make in product work.
